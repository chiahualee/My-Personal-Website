With my dearest friends in Taipei

by Chia Hua Lee

It is unbelieveble to have all these ppl around in Taipei and here we are at Ding Tai Fung. (Left to right: Kate、Alfie、Camille、Michelle and Me! )

It has been more than a year (even more for Kate and Camille) since we all left Germany for our routine life back in our own country. Time never ceases for us; we jsut have to push ourselves to move forward. In the past one year, two of us are done with bachelor degree, another two has a stable job in Taipei and I decided to extend another year in university.

Here, I really want to talk about a person, as she is always the role model to me. It's Camile Dytan, the girl in the middle of the photo. Camille has earlier been hired by a logisitics company in San Francisco with a decent pay that is unimaginable for any Taiwanese fresh graduate ($58000/year), needless to mention why she is now able to afford a brand new car and move into a even bigger apartment. In fact, at the time when she was still in school, she was already capable of being financially independent. She had a 4 year full scholarship for tution and worked part-time jobs for cost of living whereas I counted everything on my parents, even till now. We are the same age, but she encountered much more than I had. She is the person I admire and who I wish to keep abreast of. I write this down because I want to remind myself not to stay too cozily in my comfort zone.

On 17th of July we hopped on a train and headed east to Pingxi where people get to put up lanterns and see the Shifen Waterfall. This is a photo taken not on purpose on our way back to Pingxi old street.

I can only say this is one of the coolest photos we had hahaha!They seriously had no idea what they were doing :P The historic building behind them is Longshan Temple.

We always take ppl to Huaxi Street for snake soup but I never tried any. They described the texture of snake meat is somehow simliar to the mixture of chicken and fish.

I bought a bag of animal shaped egg pancakes for two of us in Shilin Night Market.
